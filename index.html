Eight things that hold for every piece.

N°01
Numbered
Every garment is numbered by hand. 1 of 50, signed on a satin label.
N°02
Named
The artist's name lives on the certificate card. The piece is theirs, not ours.
N°03
Shared
Every artist receives a share of every sale. Buying is a direct transaction.
N°04
Made in London
Designed, cut and finished in London. Cotton milled in Leicester. Water-based ink.
N°05
Never repeated
Fifty pieces. Never made again. The drop closes when it closes.
N°06
Repaired for life
Bring it back. We repair it, re-stamp it, and record the repair on the line.
N°07
Inclusive by design
Soft 265gsm cotton, wider neckline, no neck labels. Made to be worn by every body.

Ethically transparent
You see where every penny goes — cotton, cut, print, artist's share. No greenwash.
