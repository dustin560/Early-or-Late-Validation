Privacy & Cookies.

How we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit earlyorlatevalidation.netlify.app and the Early or Late brand site.

Who we are

Unicorn United Ltd, the data controller for Early or Late.

Company No. 09522884 · 26 Blyth Road, London E17 8HR

Contact: hello@early-or-late.com

1 · What this page covers

This notice explains what personal data we collect about you when you visit our brand validation site, why we collect it, how we use it, who we share it with, and the rights you have under UK GDPR and the EU GDPR. It also explains how we use cookies and similar technologies.

2 · What we collect

From your direct input

  • Demographic information — age band and gender, collected at the entry of the survey.
  • Email address — collected only if you choose to sign up for early access.
  • Survey answers — your reactions to brand propositions, advertisement mockups, t-shirt designs, motivations, price preferences, and other questions you choose to answer.
  • Location — your selected region (UK / Europe / Rest of world), if provided at the exit gate.
  • Free-text responses — any open-text you contribute, such as your motivations in your own words or your final thoughts.

Collected automatically

  • Behavioural data — how you move through the page (mouse path, sections visited, time per section, scroll depth, clicks).
  • Session recordings — anonymous video-style replays of your session, captured by Microsoft Clarity. Recordings exclude form-field inputs, which Clarity masks by default.
  • Device data — your device type, browser, viewport size, country (from IP address — IP is not stored).
  • Referrer — where you came from before reaching our site.

3 · Why we use it

We use this information for one purpose: to validate whether the Early or Late brand proposition resonates with its intended audience. Specifically:

  • To understand which brand promises and ad creatives perform best.
  • To learn what motivates people to wear independent-artist-led fashion.
  • To shape our pricing, our packaging, and our retail strategy.
  • To build a list of people who want first access to our launch drop.

We do not use your data to advertise to you directly until you sign up for early access. We do not sell or rent your data to anyone.

4 · Legal basis for processing

Under UK GDPR, we rely on:

  • Consent — for behavioural analytics (Microsoft Clarity session recordings) and for marketing emails. You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interest — for the core survey functionality (Netlify Forms processing the survey submission you actively send), which is the reason you visited the site.

5 · Who we share it with

We use three third-party processors. Each is bound by their own data-protection commitments:

  • Netlify, Inc. — hosts the website and processes survey form submissions. Data is stored in the United States under standard contractual clauses for international transfer.
  • Microsoft Clarity — captures anonymous session recordings and aggregate behavioural data, only if you have consented to analytics cookies. Microsoft's Clarity privacy notice is at privacy.microsoft.com.
  • Email service providers — if you sign up for early access, your email is stored in our database (via Netlify Forms) and processed by our email service provider when we send you the drop notification.

We do not share your data with any other third party. We do not run advertising trackers from Meta, Google, or any other ad network on this site.

6 · How long we keep it

  • Survey responses (anonymous) — retained for 24 months for research and benchmarking purposes, after which they are aggregated and the individual records deleted.
  • Email addresses — retained until you unsubscribe, or for 24 months after your last interaction with our communications, whichever comes first.
  • Session recordings — retained by Microsoft Clarity for 30 days, then automatically deleted.

7 · Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Ask for a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct anything that is wrong.
  • Ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Ask us to restrict how we use your data.
  • Object to our processing on the basis of legitimate interest.
  • Ask us to send your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent to analytics or marketing at any time.

To exercise any of these, email hello@early-or-late.com with the request. We will respond within 30 days.

8 · Cookies and similar technologies

We use two categories of cookies:

Strictly necessary

These make the site function — for example, remembering your survey progress within a single session. They cannot be disabled. They do not track you across sites and are not used for advertising.

Analytics (optional, requires your consent)

Microsoft Clarity uses cookies to record anonymous session replays and produce aggregate behaviour metrics (where people scroll, click, hesitate). These cookies are not set unless you accept the analytics consent banner on your first visit. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this site.

9 · Cross-border data transfer

Some of our processors are based outside the UK and EU (Netlify and Microsoft in the US). Where data is transferred internationally, we rely on the UK-EU adequacy decision or on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the UK ICO and the European Commission.

10 · Complaints

If you have a concern about how we have used your personal data and we have not resolved it to your satisfaction, you have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office:

  • Online: ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint
  • Phone: 0303 123 1113

11 · Changes to this notice

We will update this page if our practices change. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent version.
